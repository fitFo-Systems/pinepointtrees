## The Estimate Tool

This is what sets Pine Point apart from other tree service websites in the area.

### How It Works

- Homeowner selects a service, then answers targeted questions specific to that service type
- **Tree Removal:** tree count, height, nearby hazards, truck access, stump removal needed
- **Trimming:** tree count, main concern (overhang, shaping, deadwood, clearance), height, access
- **Stump Grinding:** stump count, diameter size, access, replant intent
- **Lot Clearing:** lot size, brush vs. trees density, equipment access, end goal
- Gets an instant price range — low, typical, high
- No phone call needed, no contact form required first

### After the Estimate

Two clear next steps:

- **Schedule a Follow-Up** — opens a quick form (name, phone, preferred time window). We call you back to discuss and set up a free on-site visit.
- **Call Now** — tap to call directly

### Custom Stump Carving

- Separate request form for creative work that can't be priced online
- Customer describes their idea and uploads photos of the stump
- You review and follow up

### Pricing — Proof of Concept

- Current numbers are based on general industry ranges, not your actual completed jobs
- Before going live, we want to compare against a handful of your recent invoices and adjust wherever they feel off
- The system is designed to improve over time as real job data feeds back in

## What Happens When Someone Submits

When a homeowner submits an estimate, schedules a follow-up, or sends a carving request:

- Their details go to a **Google Sheet** you can check on your phone anytime
- You get an **email notification** with their name, phone, job details, and any photos they uploaded
- Photos are saved in a **Google Drive folder** organized by submission
- Cost: $0/year — all Google services on the free tier
