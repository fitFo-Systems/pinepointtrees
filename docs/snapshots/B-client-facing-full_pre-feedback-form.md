## The Estimate Tool

This is what sets Pine Point apart from other tree service websites in the area.

### How It Works

- Homeowner answers 5 questions (service type, tree count, height, access, hazards)
- Gets an instant price range — low, typical, high
- No phone call needed, no contact form required first
- Can optionally leave their info and upload photos of the job

### Photo Uploads

- Available on all estimate requests, not just carving
- Helps you understand the job before you call back
- Messaging: "helps us understand the job before we come out"

### Custom Stump Carving Path

- Separate form for creative work that can't be priced online
- Customer describes their idea and uploads stump photos
- You review and follow up

### Pricing — Proof of Concept

- Current numbers are based on general industry ranges, not your actual jobs
- Before going live, we want to compare against your recent invoices
- The system improves over time as real job data feeds back in
- Low-variation work (stump grinding) converges fastest
